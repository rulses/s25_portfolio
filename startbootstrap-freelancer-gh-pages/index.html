Business Analytics and Information Systems

Key courses included Computational Thinking, Database Management, Data Mining, Data Wrangling, Marketing Analytics, Digital Product Management, and a Business Analytics Capstone, all of which helped build my ability to analyze data, derive insights, and support data-driven decision-making.

Marketing

As a Marketing student, I gained a deep understanding of the philosophy and core activities of marketing, as well as the external and internal environments that shape marketing strategy. I studied buyer behavior, decision-making frameworks, and developed skills in analyzing marketing problems using spreadsheets and data-driven approaches.
